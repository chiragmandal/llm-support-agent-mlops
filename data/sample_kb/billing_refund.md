# Billing & Refund Policy

Refund eligibility:
- Monthly plans: refunds within 7 days of first purchase if usage is under 20%.
- Annual plans: refunds within 14 days if usage is under 10%.
Process:
1) Ask for order ID and account email.
2) Verify eligibility and confirm refund method (original payment).
3) Refunds take 5–10 business days depending on the bank.
